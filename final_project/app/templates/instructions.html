Before

Thank you for volunteering to order lab lunch! A day or two before the lunch, send an e-mail to labfriday [at] lists.berkeley.edu letting everyone know there will be food on Friday. Food is typically ordered as one of the two options:

  • Pizza: The easiest thing to order is a bunch of pizzas because the order can be placed on the morning-of. Popular places to order from are La Val's or West Coast Pizza. Gauge attendance beforehand so you can order an appropriate number of pizzas, and be sure to always include a couple vegetarian options.
  • Individual: Set-up a custom group order using DoorDash, Caviar, or another food delivery service, and e-mail out a group ordering link at least a day before the lunch. Pick a place keeping the budget in mind; a popular favorite is Stuffed Inn.

Day-Of

When the food arrives (typically around noon) - either by delivery or by pick-up - e-mail the list again. Then enjoy the lunch! At the end of the day, make sure all the leftovers are claimed or thrown away.

After

In order to be reimbursed for the lunch, obtain your funding codes from the PI or someone else in the group. Then follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the Entertainment Reimbursement Form (fill out the PAYEE, EVENT PURPOSE, ATTENDEES & COSTS, COA, and HOST CERTIFICATION sections). Sign and date the form, and get the PI (or relevant purchaser) to sign it too.
  2. Scan the form and your receipt for the lunch into ONE single PDF document.
  3. Log-into BearBuy.
    • Click SHOP (the shopping cart) on the left, and then VIEW FORMS (under "Go To").
    • Click VIEW FORM for the Campus Shared Services form.
    • Fill out your name and e-mail, leaving the department information blank.
    • Select the activity type (entertainment). Scroll down and fill out the brief description under "item information" (include a couple sentences about the purpose of the lunch - for example "for group bonding and to share latest research statuses"), but leave the total estimated amount as $1.00 (why? I have no idea).
    • Attach your PDF document as an "internal attachment".
    • Click GO at the top of the page.
    • Give the cart a name so you can find it later if needed.
    • Click VIEW CART DETAILS.
    • Enter the ORGNODE number.
    • Click the ACCOUNTING CODES tab and enter the appropriate accounting codes.
    • Click FINAL REVIEW and then SUBMIT at the top.
  4. Reimbursement can take anywhere from a few days to a few weeks, but you can always check on the cart's approval status on BearBuy in the meantime (DOCUMENTS tab, then SEARCH DOCUMENTS). You may get an e-mail from the BearBuy admin staff requesting further details if there are any issues.
